Reason 1: Human-focused design

Radically original composition with comfort & experience at its core.

What I love most about my AirPods Max is their incredible design. From the cushion to the canopy, every part is crafted for an uncompromising fit. They create the perfect acoustic seal that fits comfortably on my head and fully immerses me in every sound and allows me to listen to my music for a long time.

The canopy spanning the headband is made from a breathable knit mesh, distributing weight to reduce on‑head pressure.
The stainless steel frameis wrapped with a soft-to-the-touch material for a remarkable combination of strength, flexibility, and comfort.
Telescoping arms smoothly extend and stay where you set them, for a consistent fit and seal.
The beautifully anodized aluminum cups feature a revolutionary mechanism that allows each cup to rotate independently and balance pressure.
